We exist to assist organizations, firms and individuals achieve outstanding performance - on terms they define!!!
BAC Insights, also known legally as Bob Angell Consulting Ltd., is the business, advisory and investment vehicle for Ian, Barb Angell and friends. The firm was established to encapsulate years of knowledge and experience, to provide advice, and, where needed and helpful, to back up that advice with investment.
MEET OUR TEAM
Robert Ian Angell
Founder and Chair

Robert Ian Angell realized that the world of financial and growth strategies was a hot mess while working for a big international corporation. Seeking to make it easier for companies worldwide to make sense of data, he set on a mission to change the world of strategic advisory and consulting

Bob was initially just one man with a laptop and a desire to make people understand financial strategy better and make more informed choices in their investments. Quickly, what was meant to be a side project grew into a viable business with a handful of employees. At first, Bob's main focus was financial analysis, mergers and acquisitions, and investment advice, but slowly the team grew and could focus more on projects requiring intellectual property protection and defence and organic and inorganic growth for the companies.
